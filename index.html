What is it?

A possible chance to meet the love of your life? Or just a fun different way to meet new people.

Speed dating involves a lot of mini dates with a lot of other single people. A date lasts 3-4 minutes and when its over you will switch your date partner. By the end of the night you will have had 8-12 mini dates. If you like someone, just note it down on your scorecard and if you match with them their contact details will be given to you following day.

Even if you're not really looking for someone to date, it's a great evening out idea that you can do with a friend and make new friends.

The Venue

The events will be held at Sir Tobys Hostel. The Garden is very suited to a speed dating event, and a pleasant location. There are individual 2 person tables to make it very date-like appropriate. If there are weather issues, or it starts getting dark too early in the evening, the event will be held in the downstairs area and bar.

The bar will be open during the event, and will be a place to hang out before, inbetween and after the event. If you come on your own you can settle your nerves by taking a drink and chat to the other people who you won't be dating.
